About

In the physical world, I work at the intersection of finance and technology; behind the screen, I am more of an observer moving between the flows of capital, code and data.
Starting from finance and economics, I gradually stepped into the digital world, trying to use a bit of rationality to unpack stories that are carefully packaged as "cool".
This site is a quiet corner for me in the cyber noise, where I park thoughts that may not look "cool", but feel closer to who I am.

从金融学与经济学作为起点，慢慢走进数字化世界，用一点理性去拆解那些被包装得很“酷”的故事。
